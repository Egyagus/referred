Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Information We Collect

When you submit a referral request through our website, we collect the following information:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Target company and desired role
  • LinkedIn profile URL (if provided)
  • Resume file (if uploaded)
  • Any additional notes you include

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Match you with a referrer at your target company
  • Facilitate the referral process on your behalf
  • Communicate with you about your referral request

Information Sharing

We share your information only with the referrer at your target company who will submit the referral on your behalf. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to complete the referral process. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

Our referral form is processed through FormSubmit, a third-party form handling service. Please refer to their privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, contact us at hello@referred.com.
